### STEP-BY-STEP SETUP

#### Step 1: Sign Up for Wave
1a. Go to [Waveapps.com](https://www.waveapps.com/).
1b. Click "Sign up" at the top right corner.
1c. Follow the prompts and select "Free plan" when prompted.

What you'll see: A simple sign-up form with fields for your email, password, and business name. Once you submit it, you'll land on the Wave dashboard.

#### Step 2: Create an Account on Zapier
1a. Go to [Zapier.com](https://zapier.com/).
1b. Click "Sign up" in the top left corner.
1c. Sign in with your Google account or other preferred method if you choose not to use it.

What you'll see: A welcome screen that asks about apps you use. You can skip this for now and start creating a Zap.

#### Step 3: Set Up Your First Zap
1a. Click the orange "Create" button (top left of the screen).
1b. Click "Zaps" from the dropdown menu.

What you'll see: A blank automation builder with two sections labeled "Trigger" and "Action."

#### Step 4: Configure the Trigger
1a. Click the "Trigger" box.
1b. In the search bar, type "Google My Business."
1c. Click on "Google My Business," then choose "New Review Requested."

What you'll see: Recent examples of new review requests appear below.

#### Step 5: Connect Your Google My Business Account
1a. Click "Connect" next to your chosen trigger.
1b. Authorize Zapier by clicking "Sign in to [Google My Business]."

What you'll see: A screen that asks for permission to access your Google My Business account. Once authorized, you can close the window.

#### Step 6: Configure the Action
1a. Click the "Action" box.
1b. In the search bar, type "Send Email."
1c. Click on "SendGrid" or another email service provider that you prefer.

What you'll see: The SendGrid app appears in your list of options.

#### Step 7: Set Up Your Email Template
1a. Choose "SendEmail."
1b. Fill in the details for the email, including recipient (use {{reviewer.email}} as the placeholder), subject line, and body text.
1c. Click "Test action" to see a preview of your email.

What you'll see: A test email that looks like it would be sent to the reviewer. Adjust any fields as needed.

#### Step 8: Publish Your Automation
1a. Once you're happy with the test, click "Publish."

What you'll see: A confirmation message saying your automation is now live and active.

### WHAT TO DO IF SOMETHING GOES WRONG

- **Problem:** You can't connect to Google My Business.
  - Solution: Check that you have given Zapier permission to access your account. Go back and try signing in again.

- **Problem:** The test email doesn't send.
  - Solution: Ensure all fields are correctly filled out, especially the recipient's email address. Also, check if SendGrid or any other email service is configured properly.

- **Problem:** The trigger isn't triggering.
  - Solution: Make sure you have enabled notifications in Google My Business and that your Zapier settings are correct.
